Venezuelan Hot Dogs offers two types of hot dogs with a strong brand identity built on quality ingredients and services to meet customer needs through taste, fast service and convenience. The business is owned by Lucas, who also serves as President, with Raul as Vice President and Andres as Operations Manager. Their pricing strategy focuses on providing higher prices than other fast food restaurants but still offering competitive prices compared to other gourmet options while their distribution will be done exclusively at their store located in Sawgrass mall’s food corner. Inventory management will be handled by analyzing weekly sales data stored at their small facility dedicated exclusively for frozen food storage located near Doral area. Employee training plans have been implemented following standards set by owners while future expansion plans involve opening six more stores over next year throughout Miami area targeting Venezuela community specifically through great packaging design & convenience served faster than traditional fast-food restaurants.
