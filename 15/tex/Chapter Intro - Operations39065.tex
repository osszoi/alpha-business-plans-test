

Venezuelan Hot Dogs is a Limited Liability Company that has been in operation for 4 years. Founded by Lucas, the company has 5 employees and offers two types of hot dogs: hot dog 1 and hot dog 2. Venezuelan Hot Dogs provides customers with taste, speed and convenience, setting it apart from its competitors through local adaptation catering towards its target audience –the Venezuelan community- with pricing strategies set at higher than average fast food prices for this sector. The main competitors are all the food restaurants in the Sawgrass Mall, with an estimated market size of 30 billion dollars. There is high competition, low prices and supply chain pressure within the industry which creates competitive pressure from price competition, product differentiation and marketing strategies. Currently there are 10 employees working at Venezuelan Hot Dogs but plans for hiring more family members from within the Venezuelan Community exist along with HR policies set according to law regarding hourly employees who will receive training based on company standards when hired . Physical facilities currently consist of one small location used to prepare food located in Doral area along with one small store located in Sawgrass Mall although expansion plans involve opening 6 more stores next year followed by 6 stores per year thereafter throughout Miami area . Inventory management includes having an area dedicated to frozen foods bought weekly based on sales analysis while operational processes involve 3 suppliers providing sausages , one supplying bread while sauces & other ingredients being bought from Costco . 

In summary , Venezuelan Hot Dogs is a successful business that has been able to differentiate itself from its competitors through local adaptation catering towards its target audience –the venezuelan community-with pricing strategies set higher than average fast food prices for this sector distributed mainly via its store located in Sawgrass mall plus additional expansion plans involving six new stores every year throughout Miami area. The management team consists of Lucas (President), Raul (Vice President), Andres (Operations Manager) as well as five current employees who are supported by HR policies set according to law regarding hourly employees who will receive training based on company standards when hired .
