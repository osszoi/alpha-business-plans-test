

\subsection{Yearly Cash Flow}\label{sec:title}
\nonumsubsection*{Summary} This chapter provides a detailed analysis of the yearly cash flow for Alpha Project. It examines the net income, depreciation and amortization, working capital, operating cash flow, capital expenditures from bank payment/loan and investments, net cash, and cash balance for each year. The results indicate that Alpha Project had positive net income in all years with increasing amounts over time. The company also had positive operating cash flows in all years with increasing amounts over time. These trends are likely due to increased sales revenue and improved operational efficiency as the company grows. Additionally, there were no capital expenditures from bank payments or loans in any year; however, there were significant investments made in Year 1 and Year 2 which allowed Alpha Project to continue its growth trajectory in subsequent years.
